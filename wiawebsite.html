Word In Action Website Development

Project Introduction

Word In Action Ministry is a church that I have been a member of for as long as I can remember. I was honored with the opportunity to create a website for them with the task of making online membership readily available and accesible.

Wireframes

My main focus with the moodboard was to extend upon the existing logo, using the same colors and font family. To decide on the additional colors, I combined colors seen in the physical building, creating a cohesive and familiar palette.

Completed Website



The end result has Online Membership as the center of the navigation bar, leading the user to a step-by-step process of becoming an online member. The remainder of the website encourages others to view the church service, stay updated with the church's social media accounts, and come to events.

Reflection

This project was a big step, giving me experience in client-work and using Wix as a Web Development tool. I learned to turn client wants and needs into an interactive design, keeping in mind the main goals of the company as well as their budget. This allowed me to utilize elements such as calendar management and google forms for easy use and access for Church Leaders.

One of the main things I focused on for this project was accessibility and user-friendliness. For church organizations, people of all ages and backgrounds need to be able to use and understand the website. This challenged my thinking, helping me to iterate over my designs until landing on a balanced, user-friendly design.

I am excited to publish this website as my first client-project and look forward to further improvements and iterations as I gain more skill and knowledge.
